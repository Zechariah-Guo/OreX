About OreX

What is OreX?

OreX is a Minecraft-themed stock market simulation where you trade ores like stocks. Each ore has unique market characteristics — from stable Coal to volatile Netherite. Prices fluctuate every 20 seconds based on an algorithm that considers trends, supply and demand, bot traders, and random market events.

How It Works

  1. Create an account — You start with $10,000 in virtual currency.
  2. Browse the market — View all 9 ores with their current prices and trends.
  3. Buy ores — Purchase ores you think will increase in value.
  4. Watch the market — Prices update every 20 seconds automatically.
  5. Sell for profit — Sell your ores when the price is right.
  6. Climb the leaderboard — Compete with other traders for the highest portfolio value.

Market Mechanics

The market engine uses several factors to determine price movements:

  • Trend effect — If a price has been rising, it becomes more likely to fall (mean reversion), and vice versa.
  • Gravity effect — Prices that drift far from their base price are pulled back over time.
  • Bot traders — 9 simulated traders make decisions each tick, creating market activity.
  • Player influence — Your trades have a small effect on the next price movement.
  • Market events — Rare events (0.5% chance per tick) cause sudden 3× price movements.
  • Volatility — Each ore has a volatility rating that scales price changes.

Trading Tips

  • Diversify your portfolio across multiple ores to reduce risk.
  • Watch the trend indicators — a long streak in one direction often reverses.
  • High-volatility ores (Redstone, Netherite) offer bigger gains but also bigger losses.
  • Low-volatility ores (Coal, Diamond) are safer but grow more slowly.
  • Check the price floor and ceiling — prices cannot go beyond these bounds.
  • Use the price chart on ore detail pages to spot trends before trading.
